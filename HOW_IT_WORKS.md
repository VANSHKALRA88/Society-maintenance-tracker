# How It Works — Society Maintenance Tracker

Read this once before you open the live link. It takes 2 minutes and tells you exactly what will happen.

---

## Step 1: Create an Account

When you open the app, you'll see a **Create Account** option. Click it.

You'll be asked to choose **who you are**:
- **Society Owner** (you manage a society/building)
- **Resident** (you live in a flat and want to join a society that's already set up)

Pick the one that matches you.

---

## Step 2A: If you chose "Society Owner"

You'll fill in a few details about your society:
- Society Name
- Address
- Street
- Number of Floors

Click **Submit**.

✅ As soon as you submit, the app **instantly creates your Society ID** (a unique code/link).

You'll also need to **add at least one flat** to your society (flat number, floor, etc.) — this sets up your building structure.

### Now share your Society ID
This is the important part: **copy your Society ID** and send it to your residents (via WhatsApp, email, whatever's easiest). This ID is what lets them join *your* society specifically — no one else's.

---

## Step 2B: If you chose "Resident"

You'll be asked to **paste the Society ID** that your society owner shared with you.

Once you paste it, you're connected to the correct society. Then you'll add your own details:
- Your flat number/floor
- Basic profile info

Click **Submit** and you're in.

---

## Step 3: Dashboard

Once account setup is done (either as Owner or Resident), you land on your **Dashboard**. This is your home screen where you can:
- View/raise maintenance complaints
- Check notices posted by the society
- Track flat and payment-related details
- See updates relevant to your role

Owners see a management view (all flats, all residents, all complaints).
Residents see their own flat's view (their complaints, notices, status updates).

---

## Quick Summary (TL;DR)

```
New user
  └─ Create Account
       ├─ Society Owner → Enter society details → Submit
       │                  → Society ID auto-generated
       │                  → Add a flat
       │                  → Share Society ID with residents
       │                  → Dashboard (manage everything)
       │
       └─ Resident → Paste Society ID → Add flat details → Submit
                     → Dashboard (your flat's view)
```

That's it — now you're ready to try the live demo.
